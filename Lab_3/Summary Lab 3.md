## **Introduction**

This lab focused on understanding and implementing various IP addressing schemes including IPv4, IPv6, public, and private addresses. Emphasis was placed on configuration, subnetting, and addressing methodologies required for modern networking.

---

## **Learning Objectives**

- Explain and implement private IP addresses  
- Differentiate between IPv4 and IPv6 addressing schemes  
- Apply IPv4 subnetting solutions  
- Understand IPv6 concepts including SLAAC and EUI-64  
- Use loopback, broadcast, and default gateway IPs in troubleshooting

---

## **Lab Summary**

The lab explored four major areas:
1. IP Addressing Schemes and manual configuration  
2. Differences between IPv4 and IPv6  
3. Subnetting and binary conversion techniques  
4. Advanced IPv6 concepts including VIPs and subinterfaces
