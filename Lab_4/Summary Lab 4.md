## **Introduction**

This lab introduced various types of network media and connectors, focusing on copper, fiber, and coaxial cable types, their advantages, applications, and termination methods. Understanding these components is crucial to designing efficient and reliable networks.

---

## **Learning Objectives**

- Identify types of internet provider connections  
- Distinguish between copper and fiber cables  
- Recognize different connector types used in networking  
- Understand termination standards and transceivers  

---

## **Lab Summary**

The lab was divided into three core exercises:
1. Internet provider links and service options  
2. Cable types and construction  
3. Connectors and transceivers used for various media types  

Each section focused on real-world application of media and interface hardware in a network.
