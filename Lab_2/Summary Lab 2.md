## **Introduction**

This lab introduced the concept of network topologies and their characteristics, emphasizing both physical and logical designs. Various topologies such as mesh, star, bus, ring, and hybrid were explored using the LibreOffice Draw application. The lab aimed to enhance understanding of network layout designs and connectivity models.

## **Discussion**

The design of network topologies plays a critical role in infrastructure performance and reliability. This lab demonstrated the need to understand both hardware layout and logical data flow. LANs benefit from star topologies, while enterprise deployments might use hybrid models for resilience and scalability.
